Why Planting Trees is Important

Trees are essential for maintaining the ecological balance of our planet. They not only provide oxygen but also help purify the air, provide shelter and food for wildlife, and contribute to mitigating climate change by absorbing carbon dioxide.

How to Plant a Tree

  1. Choose a species suitable for your climate and environment.
  2. Prepare the soil by loosening it and adding nutrients if necessary.
  3. Dig a hole large enough for the tree roots.
  4. Place the tree in the hole and fill with soil, firming around the trunk.
  5. Water thoroughly.
  6. Care for and protect your tree as it grows.
